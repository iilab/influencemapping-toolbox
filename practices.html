Practices

There are a number of essential practices which most influence mapping project apply along the way.

  • Plan
  • Collect
  • Organise
  • Publish


We've organised these practices in a way that allow you to find more by looking at existing project documentation and relevant tools that can be used in the context of this practice.

Plan

When you get started on a project it's usually an intuition or a question that guides you, but to achieve your goals, a little bit of planning goes a long way.

  • Get Started: Get started on an influence mapping project
  • Project Design: Define your problem and structure your project
  • Ethics: Do the right thing
  • Collaboration: Get the right skillset in your team
  • Planning: Devise a way to achieve your goals

Organise

Investigating, analysing and finding more about your problem goes hand in hand with how you organise and structure your data. It determines how your project is experienced by your audience but it will also have a lot of impact on the type of technology you'll need.

  • Get Started: Getting to know your data. Are the answers to your questions or the links you want to put in the spotlight in your data?
  • Model Data: How to choose a model to organise your data
  • Integrate Data: Transforming and joining data from different sources
  • Analyse Data: How to analyse your data?
  • Host & Secure Data: Where is your data going to be stored and who will be able to access it

Publish

Putting your story out there is when it can be able to create impact. But with data stories, allowing others to build on your result can be the difference between satisfying accolades by your peers and actual real world impact.

  • Get Started: What are the ways in which your result and your data can have the impact you want?
  • Present Data: Make your data tell its story and engage your audience
  • Describe Data: Explain where your data is coming from and how its structure
  • Facilitate Data Reuse: Make it easier for others to reuse and build on your data
  • Measure Impact: Know when you're having the impact you were hoping for
